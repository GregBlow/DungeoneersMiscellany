====== Subversion ======
Created Monday 08 July 2019

Subversion of expectation, e.g. revelation of an NPCs motivations being diamterically opposed to what is presumed or initially indicated.

Pros: Helps avoid predictable plots and maintain a sense of being part of a world.

Cons: If taken to excess can drift into contrariness and thus arbitrariness or predictability.
